Meet our teachers

All of our teachers have Masters Degrees in Computer Science. But we are a diverse group, with a wide variety of undergraduate degrees in Physics, Psychology, Engineering, Mathematics and even Architecture.

The beauty of programming is that it is universally useful. Our teachers know this first hand having written programmes that model physics particles, map aerodynamics in crowded cities, read brain signals, use artificial intelligence to classify the complex real world, design buildings and complex web systems, connect the worlds emerging economies to banks.
