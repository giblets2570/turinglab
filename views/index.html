About

A little about our journey and where we're headed

  • 2011-2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Most of our team has been independantly tutoring throughout university to engage a new generation of thinkers in different backgrounds; computer science, maths, physics, architecture, design and psychology.

  • September 2014

    A new teaching environment

    Our co-founders met at Imperial College London on the Computer Science Masters conversion course, brought together by a common aim: to teach a new generation.

  • June 2015

    Teaching

    Starting to teach through Imperial College at the Coder-Dojo's. Expanding to Schools.

  • September 2015

    Full Time

    Our full term programm will begin with a number of schools, and independant groups. Along side this, our team will be developing and improving a creative approach to teaching computer science that engages students of all ages.
